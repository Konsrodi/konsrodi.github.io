About me

I am Konstantina Rodi and I was born in Patras in 1991. From an early age, I was greatly interested in computers and the way they function and perform. During my school days I was able to deal with IT issues and after attending the Technical Professional/Vocational Lyceum in Patras, I was able to expand my skills and receive further training on Specialty Support Systems, Applications and Networks.

Following this dream of mine, I have recently graduated from the Department of Computer Engineering & Informatics at the Technological Educational Institute of Western Greece after successfully completing my thesis on networks (Networks and their Applications). Furthermore, I had the opportunity of completing my internship at the Computer Technology Institute and Publications as technical support assistant at the Information System myschool (May-November 2016).

I have a lot of hobbies which mostly include reading, watching movies, dancing, listening to good music, writing articles, watching theatrical perfomances. Right now,i write articles for Artic.gr and i love it. I also love volunteering and i have participated in Patras IQ (2016) with e-Business & UX Lab of Technological Educational Institute of Western Greece, 20th Panhellenic Conference on Informatics(2016), and recently at 1st Patras Science Festival (2017).

Last, my research interests are:

  • Development of Web systems

  • Networks and their applications

  • Robotics

  • Human Computer Interaction
